Welcome

Salon Solace is a calm and friendly salon with a zen-like atmosphere. We share our many years of experience in the beauty industry while providing personal service. Salon solace is a place where you can escape the hustle and bustle of your day to relax and recharge. You’ll feel beautiful and your spirit will feel lifted. That’s why we call it a place of quiet beauty.

About Darlene Morton

“The satisfaction of making someone look and feel beautiful continues to be deeply fulfilling.”

Hi. My name is Darlene Morton and I’m the Owner and Founder of Salon Solace. Even after 35 years in the beauty industry, the satisfaction of making you feel and look beautiful continues to be deeply fulfilling. I welcome you to our place of quiet beauty, Salon Solace.

My journey in the beauty industry began over 35 years ago, starting as a Hair Stylists’ Assistant, learning the dos and donts of mixing color formulas, applications, and finishing. After a year and a half, I moved to a salon that was ALL about education. There I honed my skills and became one of their Hair Cutting Trainers. Eventually, I was Assistant Manager for several years until it was time to start my family.

Fast forward about 10 years when I met someone with a similar passion and approach to the beauty industry. She became a fast friend. And with her, we co-created The Hamptons Salon, a beautiful, tranquil salon where we enjoyed over 15 successful years of business. After 15 years it was time to move on and try something new. Which leads me to Salon Solace! I can’t wait for you to see the space and experience our zen-like atmosphere.

In addition to Hair Stylist, I’m also a Yoga Instructor. I’ve always turned to movement as a stress reliever, but when I took a yoga class, I was hooked because it added so much peace and groundedness to my life. As a result, I became a certified Yoga Instructor and I conduct small group classes when my schedule allows.

If you are interested in small group yoga, email me here. 

About Barbara Paulo

“I create an atmosphere where each client feels welcomed, pampered and genuinely cared for. It’s my goal to always exceed your expectations. Hair styling is my passion and art.”

Hello, my name is Barbara Paulo. I am very happy to join Salon Solace as a Master Colorist and Creative Designer. I am a native to the Pioneer Valley. After many years experience as a professional stylist and salon owner, in 2003 I founded Shear Xtreme Salon where I created an atmosphere in which each client feels welcomed, pampered and genuinely cared for. It’s my goal to always exceed your expectations. Hairstyling is my passion and art.

I also have a team of expert stylists, Beauty to Be. As your wedding or special event needs arise, we can assist by being your on and off-site hair and make-up team. Our team makes it a priority to undergo continuous education, certification and training to keep current on the latest styles and trends. We will always remain focused on your individual needs by taking the time to listen and make your vision a reality.

I invite you to sit back and enjoy a quiet moment away from your busy day in my chair. I can create your new look and teach you how to maintain that style with the right products and tools. I would be honored to have you as my guest in the new beautiful location at Salon Solace. Hope to meet you soon!
