What Is Salon Solace?

Salon Solace is a friendly salon in Easthampton, Massachusetts. We’ve created a zen-like modern atmosphere within a former industrial mill building. We take the time to provide personal service, sharing our many years of experience in the beauty industry. Salon Solace is a place where you can escape the hustle and bustle. At Salon Solace, you’ll relax and recharge, feeling beautiful and lifted. That’s why we call it a place of quiet beauty.

Where are you?

Salon Solace is in located in Easthampton, Massachusetts in a former mill building called the Keystone Mill. The address is 122 Pleasant Street and we are in Suite 130. Detailed directions are on our Contact Page.
